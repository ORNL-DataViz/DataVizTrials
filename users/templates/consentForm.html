Binary Image Processing Across Different Technologies

Introduction

We are inviting you to take part in a research study about the design of user interfaces among different devices for the task of image tagging. We are interested in how our user interface designs for each device differs between virtual reality, augmented reality, tablets, and standard monitor set ups. The person in charge of this study is Toni Pence, Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at the University of North Carolina Wilmington. Your participation consists of a pre-questionnaire, performing tasks by interacting with different user interface designs, post-questionnaire, and a debriefing. Your participation is expected to be around 30 minutes and no more than 1 hour

Your participation is voluntary. You may refuse to answer any question. You may stop at anytime without penalty.
